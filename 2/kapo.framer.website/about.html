Our vision is to make Make design accessible to everyone

We’ve never established a physical workspace — and don't anticipate ever doing so. Our thriving team of 125 is scattered across 27 nations, operating from the comfort of their abodes. Kapo, our creation, is designed with a transparent file system allowing all to transition their work into alternate applications — and encourages others to engineer fresh tools and processes revolving around us.

We’ve consistently offered our commodity at a reasonable rate. This strategy has sustained our autonomous enterprise for more than a decade. Our intention remains to persist for many more years.

It all started with an idea that became a family

Our founding partners, Danny and Samantha, are still actively involved. In addition to steering our product, they make sure we adhere to our principles and practice what we preach. As Kapo continues to develop, our aim is to make the process of generating impressive designs even more seamless. And as we expand, we'll keep doing things the way we know best — designing useful features, addressing genuine issues, and enabling everyone to design extraordinary things.
